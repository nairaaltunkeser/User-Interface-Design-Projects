What is Aperture?

Aperture can be defined as the opening in a lens through which light passes to enter the camera.

It is an easy concept to understand if you just think about how your eyes work.

As you move between bright and dark environments, the iris in your eyes either expands or shrinks, controlling the size of your pupil.
